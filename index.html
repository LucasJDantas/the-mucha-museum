The
Museum

The only museum in the world dedicated to the life and work of the world-acclaimed Czech ART NOUVEAU artist Alphonse Mucha (1860 - 1939) was opened in Prague to the general public on 13 February 1998. The area of 500 m2 of Mucha Museum also includes the Museum Shop, which offers an exclusive selection of gifts & posters with Alphonse Mucha motifs.

Exhibition

The museum is divided into seven sections exhibiting

  • Decorative panels
  • Parisian posters
  • Documents décoratifs
  • Czech Posters
  • Drawings and pastels
  • Paintings
  • Studio and photographs
